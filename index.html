Tayo Conga Africa

Ajayi Oluwatayo Oluwadamilare who goes by the popular stage name, Tayo Conga is a native of Erugbe, Ikogosi in Ekiti West Local Government of Ekiti state. Tayo Conga holds a degree in Philosophy from Olabisi Onabanjo University ,Ago-Iwoye ,Ogun State . After his university education ,he was posted to serve the one-year compulsory National Youth Service Corp (NYSC) at the Tourism Board of Delta State.

On completion of his service year, he joined a Debt recovery firm, where he worked as the Operations Officer briefly before switching fully to his passion in entertainment and tourism Tayo Conga is presently a member of the music department of the Elevation Church in Lekki, Lagos where he contributes to the ministration of music with his prolific percussion skills.

ABOUT TAYO CONGA

  • MY PHILOSOPHY
  • Vision
  • MY ENGAGEMENTS

Tayo Conga has an energtic and African Aura

HAPPINESS– To put smiles on people's faces at all times through the power of the 'Conga'.

Tayo Conga aims to spotlight and propagate the African Traditional music using percussion.

He also aims to infuse the current West African Sounds (Afropop) with the African Traditional music (percussion).

He strives to further spotlight the African Traditional sounds (percussion) on the world map.

Tayo Conga has at different times worked with various Nigerian 'A-list' artistes of gospel and secular music. Some of these include Flavour, Wizkid, Banky W, Nathaniel Bassey, Sammie Okposo, Debo Ojubuyi (Zion Ilaje) among others. He has also featured at the back end of several music video productions and live concerts.
